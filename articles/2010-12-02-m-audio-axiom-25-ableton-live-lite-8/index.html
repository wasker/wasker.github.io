M-Audio Axiom 25 + Ableton Live Lite 8

Written by wasker

Got lovely Axiom 25 Gen 2 as a holiday gift? Don’t be upset with DirectLink not working: the problem is with Live’s support of DirectLink in version 8, it’s broken - Ableton’s Live 8.2.2 beta fixes it.

And you have to install M-Audio’s drivers 1.1.2+ - the ones that come with Windows do not support DirectLink.
